About Event

1- First step is an event for students who have just finished high school and joined the  Faculty of Computers and Artificial Intelligence that tell them about the faculty and it’s departments and it’s fields which also tell them about the difference between school life and the university life and the advantages, disadvantages, and skills that will be learned from the faculty.
2- First step will be the start of the journey to university life and learning in the faculty of computer and information science and the start of his journeys in FCAI.

Goals

1- Help newcomers to FCAI to accept the field of computer sciences and know it’s importance.
2- Help newcomers to know their universities better and the difference between university and school.
3- Help them to know the computer science field better and what they will learn at FCAI.

Where

New York, United States

When

october. 10. 2020
